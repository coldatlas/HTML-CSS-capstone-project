How it works

PLLAY WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS.

1- SEARCH:

It's simple. On pllay homepage, enter the name of a gaming center you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all gaming centers.

2- LOCATE:

Get to see where the gaming center is located by clicking on the gaming center profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- RESERVE:

Reserve anytime to the gaming center of your choice. Click on the RESERVE button to make your reservation in advance.

4- HAVE FUN:

Once you make your reservation. You can now go at the time of your choice and have fun. You can check out our blog to know about the latest gaming centers and video games.
